Research Interests

I am interested in scalable skill acquisition for embodied agent like robots, which may enable robots mastering many skills and are capable of operating along with humans. I'm trying to accomplish such a goal by developing learning paradigms which enables us to built 1 agent by leveraing existing data (priors) from other agents, including agent with different observation and action space. I'm particularly interested in asking robots to learn from humans, who produces huge amount of data daily on social media, and online video plateform. Some technical challenges involves:

  • Representation learning: how do we represent multimodal experience from other agents?
  • Skill learning: learning skills via Reinforcement learning, Suepervised (Imitation) Learning ...
